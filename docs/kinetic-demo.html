Philosophy

Design Principles

01

Typographic Dominance

Text is the interface. Oversized headings, bold weights, mathematical spacing. Every word commands attention.

02

Kinetic Geometry

Motion with purpose. No frivolous animations. Every transition reinforces hierarchy and guides attention.

03

Stark Contrast

Pure black, pure white, electric lime. No gradients. No gray areas. Maximum visual impact through restraint.

04

Swiss Precision

8-point grid system. Mathematical spacing. Perfect alignment. Clarity through systematic constraints.

05

Editorial Boldness

Magazine-style layouts. Asymmetric composition. Generous whitespace. Confident, not timid.

06

Zero Decoration

No shadows. No glows. No rounded corners. Beauty through function, not ornament.
